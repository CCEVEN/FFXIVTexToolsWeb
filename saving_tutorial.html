Saving

You must save textures in the same format they were exported, which can be seen in the viewer pane.
Once you've modified your texture of choice in photoshop, go to File > Save As.

We then want to change Save as type: from Intel Texture Works to D3D/DDS.


The type of texture to save as can be seen in top left corner of the viewer pane.

After you click on save, you will be taken to the NVIDIA save settings screen.
