Saving

You must save textures in the same format they were exported, which can be seen in the info box.
Once you've modified your texture of choice in photoshop, go to File > Save As

We then want to change Save as type: from Intel Texture Works to D3D/DDS


The type of texture to save as can be seen in the info box in the application


After you click on save, you will be taken to the NVIDIA save settings screen.
Here are the settings for each Texture Type:

DXT1




DXT5




32bit A8R8G8B8




64bit A16B16G16R16F
